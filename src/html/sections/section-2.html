WHO WE ARE

We are NAVI - the world's leading counter strike club

Natus Vincere (Latin for "born to win"), abbreviated NAVI ( formerly Na`Vi), is a Ukrainian esports organization based in Kyiv. Founded in 2009, the organization has teams and players competing in various Video games. At NAVI, we are more than just a team; we are a legacy, a force to be reckoned with, and the heartbeat of the esports world. As the world's leading Counter-Strike club, we have set the bar high, redefining the standards of competitive gaming since our inception.
